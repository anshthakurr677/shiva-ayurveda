Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

SHIVA AYURVEDA is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

🔒 Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery address and billing details
  • Order history and purchase information
  • Messages and inquiries you send us
  • Device and browser data (for website analytics)

📋 How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates via SMS/email
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • Send wellness tips and offers (only if you opt-in)
  • Improve our website and product offerings

🤝 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share with:

  • Delivery partners — for shipping your orders
  • Payment gateways (Razorpay) — for secure payment processing
  • Government authorities — if required by law

🛡️ Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your payment details are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways (Razorpay). We do not store your card or UPI details on our servers.

🍪 Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your cart, improve your browsing experience, and analyse traffic. You can disable cookies in browser settings, though this may affect some features.

✅ Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications anytime
