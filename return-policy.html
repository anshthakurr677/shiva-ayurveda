Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

🌿 At SHIVA AYURVEDA, your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

🔄 Return Window

You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order. After 7 days, we unfortunately cannot offer a return or exchange.

✅ Eligible for Return

  • Product received is damaged or broken
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product is expired or near expiry
  • Product packaging is tampered or unsealed

❌ Not Eligible for Return

  • Products that have been opened and used
  • Return request raised after 7 days of delivery
  • Products purchased during sale or at discounted prices
  • Dissatisfaction with product results (natural products may vary person to person)

💰 Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days
  • Refund will be credited to your original payment method
  • For COD orders, refund will be done via bank transfer
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable

📦 How to Initiate a Return

  • WhatsApp or call us at +91 98765 43210 within 7 days
  • Share your order number and reason for return
  • Share photos of the damaged/wrong product
  • We will arrange a pickup or guide you for courier

🔁 Exchange Policy

We replace items only if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange a product, contact us at hello@shivaayurveda.in with your order details and photos.

📞 Contact for Returns

  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210
  • Email: hello@shivaayurveda.in
  • Working Hours: Mon–Sat, 9AM–6PM
