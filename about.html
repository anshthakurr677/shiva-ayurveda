Est. 2010 · Our Story

Rooted in Ancient
Indian Wisdom

SHIVA AYURVEDA was born from a simple belief — that nature holds the cure for every ailment. Founded in 2010 in the foothills of the Himalayas, our journey began with a deep reverence for Ayurvedic traditions passed down through generations.

The name SHIVA AYURVEDA is a tribute to the ancient vaidyas who found remedies in every leaf, root, and bark of the forest. We carry that legacy forward, blending timeless wisdom with modern science.
