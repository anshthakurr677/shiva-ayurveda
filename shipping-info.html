📍 Delivery Coverage

We deliver across India including all states and union territories. For remote areas (Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep, Ladakh), delivery may take additional 2–3 days.

⏱️ Order Processing Time

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM are dispatched the next working day
  • Orders are not processed on Sundays and public holidays

📦 Packaging

All our products are carefully packed in eco-friendly, tamper-proof packaging to ensure they reach you in perfect condition. We use minimal plastic and recyclable materials wherever possible.

🔍 Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS/WhatsApp message with your tracking details. You can track your order using the courier partner's website or by contacting us directly.

⚠️ Delivery Issues

If your order has not arrived within the expected timeframe, please:

  • Check your tracking details first
  • Ensure someone was available to receive the package
  • Contact us at +91 98765 43210 or hello@shivaayurveda.in
  • We will resolve the issue within 24–48 hours
